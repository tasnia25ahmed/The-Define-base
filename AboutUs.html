About us

At TAPCoders, our aim is to help others learn about the various elements and terms that are used for web application development and application. By teaching others these simple but frequently used terms, we hope that we can pique their interest in the web development field and help them sharpen their skills. Not only that but we also hope to create a community where there is always space for development, learning and connection through our interactive and responsive site.
